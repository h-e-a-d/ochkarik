Meet Dr. Sarah Chen

With over 15 years of experience in ophthalmology, Dr. Sarah Chen is dedicated to providing the highest quality eye care to patients of all ages.

Dr. Chen completed her medical degree at Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine and her residency in ophthalmology at Massachusetts Eye and Ear Infirmary. She specializes in cataract surgery, LASIK, and advanced glaucoma management.

Her patient-centered approach combines cutting-edge technology with compassionate care, ensuring every patient receives personalized treatment plans tailored to their unique needs.
